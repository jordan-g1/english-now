Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime directly through the App Store. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → English Now → Cancel Subscription. Canceling stops future charges; you keep access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open English Now and tap "Restore" on the paywall screen. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

The microphone isn't working — what do I do?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → make sure English Now is toggled on. If it's already on, try force-quitting the app and reopening it.

The AI isn't responding or the session froze.

This is usually a network issue. Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, force-quit the app and start a new session.

How is my English level determined?

Your level is set during onboarding based on your self-assessment. You can update it anytime in the Profile tab. The AI also adapts dynamically within sessions based on how you're performing.

I was charged but can't access the app.

Tap "Restore" on the paywall screen. If that doesn't work, email us directly with your Apple ID and we'll sort it out immediately.
